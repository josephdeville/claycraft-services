## Why Micro Influencers?

- **Higher Engagement**: Micro influencers typically see 5-10% engagement rates vs 1-3% for macro
- **Authentic Connection**: Audiences feel like "friends" with smaller creators
- **Cost Effective**: Lower collaboration costs with similar conversion rates
- **Niche Authority**: Deep expertise in specific fitness categories
- **Better ROI**: Studies show micro influencers deliver 60% higher engagement
